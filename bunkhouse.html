Your Own Little Getaway

The Bunkhouse offers customers a comfortable place to stay for a few nights, or even a few weeks. But it's more than just a hotel, it's a home away from home, where travelers can stop to rest, and locals can escape the hassles of day to day life without having to book an expensive getaway.

The Bunkhouse can sleep up to five people and is decorated with little pieces of Nashville's past. Meals are not included, but clientele receive a 10% discount at the Chuckwagon Café.



Amenities

The Bunkhouse includes a bedroom, bathroom, full kitchen, washer and dryer, and living room with two pull-out couches. Wi-fi is included and a 10% discount is offered for the Chuckwagon Café

The Bunkhouse is located right on M-66, and is a convenient spot to spend a night as you travel through Michigan.
